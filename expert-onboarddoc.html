What you need to know to get started

Do not contact candidates
We regularly check your performance (based on must haves, if you are approving candidates who do not possess the Must have requirements, you may no longer be an Expert)
How am I assigned to roles to evaluate?
  1. 1. As soon as we have a new role about to be launched, we will announce on Slack: @channel, who is available for ‘X’ role?
  2. 2. First expert to respond they are available will be selected.
  3. 3. We do keep this fair, if you know you have many roles, let someone else take it.
  4. 4. We do sometimes assign 2 Experts to 1 role, so if you respond second, we will add you on later when many submissions start coming in.
  5. 5. We will confirm on Slack that we have assigned you and just opened the role to sourcing.
  6. 6. It will now be available on your personal dashboard.
How do I get paid?
  1. 1. Your contract is through Upwork: we will invite you.
  2. 2. You will be paid per hour, on Upwork.
  3. 3. You need to use the timesheet to track your hours worked.
How do I evaluate candidates?
  1. 1. Log into your account.
  2. 2. Navigate to the top left of your dashboard and click on the menu drop down: Select ‘My Jobs’.
  3. 3. You will see the number of pending submissions to evaluate on the right hand side of each role.
  4. 4. Select the role to evaluate for by clicking on it.
  5. 5. Read the MUST HAVES
  6. 6. Read the LOCATION TO SOURCE FROM
  7. 7. Read the YEARS EXPERIENCE
  8. 8. Click on the Green ‘Evaluate Candidates’ Button
  9. 9. Select ‘Thumbs up’ OR ‘Thumbs down’ on the right hand side for each candidate
What do I look at when I assess candidates?
  1. 1. You only look at the MUST HAVES, LOCATION TO SOURCE FROM and YEARS EXPERIENCE.
  2. 2. If you notice the candidate has the email domain or recent work experience of the company hiring for this role, DQ and notify us ASAP. (This should rarely happen)
How long do I have to assess candidates?
  1. 1. 24 to 48 hours from submission date.
  2. 2. If you notice you are too busy, let us know on Slack immediately and we can add an Expert to the role to assist you
What do I do if I notice a bug/error on the platform?
  1. Please see here: How to file bugs at Visage.
What do I do if I will be travelling or if I am sick sick?
  1. 1. Notify Jeanita via email: experts.support@visage.jobs as soon as you know
  2. 2. (Jeanita will add an Expert to handle your roles during that time and block you out on the Visage calendar)
May I go back to being a recruiting sourcer if I do not enjoy being an Expert?
  1. Of course! Just let us know
